argument-hint: <company-name> [job-title]
---

Research the company provided in $ARGUMENTS for a job application. Use Tavily to gather:

1. **Company overview** — size, stage, product, market position
2. **Recent news** — last 6 months: funding rounds, layoffs, acquisitions, leadership changes, product launches
3. **Culture & reviews** — Glassdoor rating, common themes in employee reviews (search "site:glassdoor.com <company>")
4. **Blind reviews** — anonymous employee sentiment (search "site:teamblind.com <company>")
5. **Interview process** — common interview formats, technical assessment style (search "<company> interview process 2025")
6. **Salary data** — market range for the role/level (search "levels.fyi <company>" or "glassdoor <company> salary")
7. **Red flags** — any concerning signals (rapid exec departures, multiple funding delays, negative press)

Format the output as a concise report with sections for each area above. End with a **Verdict** summarising whether this looks like a strong opportunity and any questions worth asking in the interview.
